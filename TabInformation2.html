Tab Information

This tab specifies the general cement recipe (temperature, w/c, and amount of cement) and the cement composition in terms of oxides for different cement constituents.

The first group ("Recipe") defines general features of the cement mixture.  

The second group ("Aggregates") defines properties of the aggregates in the mixture. This is information is only used during transport calculatins.

The six other groups represent the ordinary Portland cement ("OPC Clinker") clinker and following supplementary cementitious materials (SCMs): granulated blast furnace slag (GBFS), silica fume (SF), fly-ash (FA), limestone (LS), and metakaolin (MK). For each material, it is possible to define oxide composition, the amount (mass%), the reacted amount (% reacted) and the reactivity of the bulk material or the oxides for the SCMs 


Recipe

General properties of the mixture.

Aggregates

Information on the aggregates in the mixture.  

OPC Clinker

Information of the OPC clinker in the mixture.

GBFS

Information of the granulated blast furnace slag in the mixture.

SF

Information of the silica fume in the mixture.

FA

Information of the fly-ash in the mixture.

LS

Information of the limestone in the mixture.

MK

Information of the metakaolin in the mixture.
