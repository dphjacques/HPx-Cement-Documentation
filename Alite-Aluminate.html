Alite - Aluminate


Benchmark description

The hydration of a strongly simplified clinker system is simulated using the modified Parrot-Killoch rate equations and thermodynamic equilibrium with cement hydrates. 

The simplified clinker system consists of 80 w% alite (C3S) and 20 w% aluminate (C4A) where C3S has 75 w% CaO and 25 w% SiO2 and C4A has 65 w% CaO and 35 w% Al2O3. The w/c ratio is 0.5.

The hydration is simulated for 10000 hours.

Volumes of solids, aqueous concentration and cumulative heat release under isothermal conditions are compared with simulations using CemGEMS (Kulik et al., 2021).
